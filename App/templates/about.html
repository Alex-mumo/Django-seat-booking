WELCOME TO DKUT BUS BOOKING

Our vision is to offer world-class transportation services in an innovative way while harnessing the use of technology to delight retain and defend our clients.

DKUT BUS BOOKING is a system that provides sudents with an online medium to get to know the car travelling at a specific time, i.e from their locality/source. to their destination. The system is run and managed by the Nyeri travel admin.

With the earlier experience where students were not able to board a bus to and fro the university, the sytem was developed to reduce that issue and enable students to book a bus and their specific travelling time.
